Privacy Policy

Sổ tay nấu ăn

Last updated: 01/02/2026

1. Introduction

Sổ tay nấu ăn ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you sign in to the app, we may collect the following information:

  • User ID (UID)
  • Email address (if provided via authentication methods)
  • Basic profile information from authentication providers

2.2 App Usage Data

We may collect non-personal usage data, including:

  • Saved recipes
  • Search history
  • In-app interactions

2.3 Analytics Data

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how users interact with the app. This may include:

  • App usage statistics
  • Device information
  • General location (country or region)

2.4 Crash Data

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and error logs to improve app stability. This data may include:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Crash traces

2.5 Advertising Data

The app displays ads using Google Mobile Ads. Google may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device identifiers
  • Ad interaction data

Advertising data is handled in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Personalize user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze usage trends and improve performance
  • Detect and fix crashes and technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Google Analytics for Firebase
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Mobile Ads

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Data may be shared only with trusted third-party services necessary for app operation, analytics, and advertising.

7. Children’s Privacy

Sổ tay nấu ăn is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Stop using the app at any time

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
